A UI & UX design student currently pursuing my bachelor's degree at MIT School of Design (Pune). My journey into the world of UX design has been a fascinating blend of curiosity, creativity, and constant learning. My work blends creativity with problem-solving, focusing on crafting meaningful solutions.

My Journey Story :

Since I was a child, creativity has been a big part of my life. I started by scribbling on the walls at home, and instead of scolding me, my parents encouraged this habit. Their support turned my interest in drawing and crafting into a lifelong passion.

In school, my teachers saw my potential and motivated me to take part in various competitions. These experiences helped me grow in confidence and made me love art even more. In junior college, I became interested in computer languages and technology, which made me think about how I could combine creativity with innovation. This led me to choose Experience Design as a career where I can use my creativity to solve real problems.

For me, design is a mixture of passion, purpose, and problem-solving.
